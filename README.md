📊 Online Retail Sales Analysis – Tata Virtual Internship Project

📌 Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the Tata Group Data Visualization Virtual Internship. The goal of the project was to analyze online retail sales data and build an interactive dashboard using Microsoft Power BI to support business decision-making.

The dashboard answers key business questions from leadership and provides insights into sales performance, customer behavior, and global product demand. Through data cleaning, transformation, and visualization, the project demonstrates how raw transactional data can be converted into meaningful business insights.

🎯 Business Questions Solved

1️⃣ Revenue Trend Analysis (CEO)

The CEO wanted to view a time-series analysis of revenue for the year 2011 to understand monthly sales patterns and seasonal trends.
A monthly revenue line chart was created to help forecast future sales performance.

2️⃣ Top Countries by Revenue (CMO)

The CMO wanted to identify the Top 10 countries generating the highest revenue along with quantity sold, excluding the United Kingdom to focus on international markets.

3️⃣ Top Customers by Revenue (CMO)

A visualization was created to show the Top 10 customers contributing the highest revenue, sorted from highest to lowest. This helps the business focus on retaining valuable customers.

4️⃣ Global Product Demand (CEO)

A global demand map was created to identify countries with the highest product demand (excluding the UK). This insight helps leadership identify potential regions for business expansion

🛠 Tools & Technologies

Microsoft Power BI – Dashboard creation and visualization

Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Calculated measures and KPIs

🧹 Data Cleaning & Preparation

The following steps were performed before analysis:

Removed negative quantities representing product returns

Filtered Unit Price greater than 0

Removed missing Customer IDs

Excluded United Kingdom in specific visuals for expansion analysis
